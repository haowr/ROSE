Our Story


Having been in operation since 1982, Rose Building Maintenance Ltd. has positioned itself as one of Alberta’s leading contract cleaning companies. Alberta owned and operated, with our head office located in St. Albert, we are able to offer professional services at competitive rates. Our management philosophy, as it relates to the provision of service, is to provide consistent prestige cleaning. In order to provide this level of service, we require clear, ongoing communication between our clients and staff at all levels, accountability, reliable resources, and in-depth knowledge of our client’s operations and personnel.

Our employees share a common goal and vision, that is, to provide the best quality workmanship in the industry. We pride ourselves on our ability to give our customers quality, scheduled service – consistently. Our management team understands the need to be able to accommodate unforeseen circumstances. With that in mind, our employees consistently demonstrate the ability to adapt to changing conditions or programs. At Rose Building Maintenance Ltd., we stress the use of environmentally friendly products and industry best practices. Additionally, we comply with all applicable W.H.M.I.S. and O.H.S. legislation.

Cleanliness equates to employee happiness and reduced employee turnover
